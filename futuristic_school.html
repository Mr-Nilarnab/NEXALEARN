Our Journey

From Vision to Reality

2018
Founded in Silicon Valley
A team of 12 educators and engineers launched NexaLearn with a bold mission: to reinvent formal education using AI and immersive technology.
2020
First AI Curriculum Launch
Deployed the world's first adaptive neural curriculum engine, personalizing learning for 2,000+ early students across 3 pilot programs.
2022
Holographic Labs Open
Inaugurated 48 holographic learning pods, enabling immersive VR/AR lab sessions that replaced traditional textbooks for Science and Engineering.
2024
Global Network Expansion
Reached 50,000 students across 60 countries, partnering with MIT, CERN, NASA, and 140 leading research institutions worldwide.
2026
Quantum Learning Initiative
Launched the world's first Quantum Computing curriculum for secondary students, giving them access to real quantum processors through cloud integration.
